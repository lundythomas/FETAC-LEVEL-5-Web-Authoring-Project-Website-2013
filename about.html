About Us

We Love Wines is a Wine Tasting business and was first set up in 1990 by Billy Bob Corker & Tina Sparkle in Dublin, Ireland as a Centre for people to come and try some exotic wines, be entertained and possibly purchase some of the fabulous wine that they have sampled.

We started off with a small premises in Dublin which we have since expanded and we now also have a large premises in North Dublin.

We cater for many different types of client including private parties, corporate events, company promotions, birthday parties, We also offer in-house wine tasting, host wine tasting parties at our 2 premises in Dublin, offer wine courses for people who would like to know more about the wines they are tasting.

We offer gift vouchers for our wine tasting experience you can give to your special someone or even as welcome gift to anyone you know that loves wine.
